            About

            Who we are and what we do.

            Who is Java_nih_deks?

            Java_nih_deks is an independent Android developer focused on building system-level optimization tools. The projects here are designed to give users genuine control over their device performance — without unnecessary complexity.

            All tools are released free of charge, with support for both rooted and non-rooted Android devices via Shizuku. New releases and updates are announced through the Telegram channel.

            What is Shizuku?

            Shizuku is an open-source Android tool that allows apps to use system APIs directly with elevated permissions — without requiring full root access. This means many performance tweaks can be applied on stock, unrooted devices.

            It works by running a privileged process via ADB or the built-in feature available on some devices (Android 11+).
